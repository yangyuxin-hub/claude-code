那件搁置的事，现在可以开始了 — 情感收尾场景

FOCAL POINT: 一扇大门正在打开，门外是明亮的光与开阔空间

ATMOSPHERE:
- 门内（左侧）：深灰背景，门边浮现消散中的标签文字："不会写代码" "不会建模" "不会设计"——文字呈灰色淡出效果
- 门外（右侧）：强烈光源，温暖白色/奶油色的开阔空间
- 一个简化人物剪影站在门槛前，面朝光，姿态是迈步向前

MOOD: 释然、可能性、开始——不是胜利感，是"原来可以"的那种轻松

COLOR TEMPERATURE: 强对比：门内冷暗（charcoal）vs 门外暖亮（cream + warm light）

LABELS: 门框上方可选标注大字："现在可以试了" 或留白由场景氛围传达

COLORS: Interior charcoal (#1A1A1A); Exterior warm cream (#F7F4EF) with bright glow; Door frame bold black; Fading labels in muted gray (#888); Figure silhouette deep black

STYLE: Editorial magazine style. High-contrast cinematic composition. Bold simplified graphic—not photorealistic, more like a powerful editorial illustration. Dramatic use of light vs dark. Human figure: simplified stylized silhouette, not photorealistic.

ASPECT: 16:9

Clean composition with strong focal point at center (the door/light). Generous negative space on the light side. Text labels should be minimal and understated. Human figures: simplified stylized silhouettes.
