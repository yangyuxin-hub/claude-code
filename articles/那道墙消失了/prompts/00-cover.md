Cover: 那道墙消失了

CONCEPT: A towering wall with a dramatic crack running through it—then dissolving, disappearing, turning to dust or light. The absence of the wall IS the subject.

COMPOSITION:
- Widescreen cinematic layout (2.35:1 ratio)
- LEFT 60%: The wall—massive, heavy, flat-vector blocks, imposing geometry—mid-collapse or dissolving from the center outward
- RIGHT 40%: Open space beyond where the wall was—bright, expansive, minimal
- Wall texture: geometric block patterns, labeled fragments dissolving: "代码" "建模" "设计" "法律" — each brick/fragment losing form, becoming particles/light
- ONE simplified human silhouette at the base, facing forward toward the open space

TITLE PLACEMENT:
- Large bold Chinese title "那道墙消失了" centered-bottom or lower-left
- Font: heavy display weight, white or near-white on dark base, or black on light—maximum contrast
- No subtitle

COLORS (mono palette, bold mood):
- Primary: Deep black (#0D0D0D) for wall structure
- Secondary: Pure white (#F9F7F4) for open space and title
- Accent: Single color pop optional — off-white or pale warm gray for particle dissolution effect
- NO gradients on solid elements; clean flat edges

STYLE: Editorial flat-vector. Bold graphic. Magazine cover quality. Strong visual tension between solid (wall) and void (open space). Clean black outlines. Minimal details. High contrast. Cinematic proportions.
