# Composition
Type composition:
- Metaphor type: Single striking visual metaphor that encapsulates the article's central irony
- The "unexpected target" — AI's crosshair lands on white-collar professionals, not blue-collar workers

Visual composition:
- Main visual: A large circular targeting reticle / bullseye (concentric rings in bold coral-red) centered slightly left. Inside the innermost ring: 2-3 simplified white-collar silhouettes (suit, briefcase, tie) — clean flat vector style, NOT photorealistic
- Background: Very dark navy-charcoal (#0D1117 to #1A1F2E) — deep, dramatic, cinematic
- Bottom-right corner: Two relaxed, untargeted silhouettes — a chef's hat figure and a worker with hard hat — slightly smaller, in muted gray tones, clearly outside the targeting rings
- Layout: Reticle occupies center-left 50% of canvas; white-collar figures inside it; blue-collar figures in peripheral safety at bottom-right; vast dark breathing space around everything
- Decorative: Thin scanning grid lines in very faint blue across the dark background, like a radar or surveillance overlay; small geometric dots suggesting data points

Color scheme:
- Background: #0D1117 deep charcoal-navy
- Target rings: Coral red #E84040, fading outward to #8B1A1A
- White-collar silhouettes: White #FFFFFF with subtle coral tint
- Blue-collar silhouettes (safe zone): Muted gray #5A5A6A
- Grid overlay: Electric blue #1A6EFF at 15% opacity
- Title text: Pure white #FFFFFF

Rendering notes: Flat vector style. Bold geometric shapes, clean hard outlines. No gradients except subtle radial glow at target center. No photorealistic textures. All figures are simplified stylized silhouettes — no facial features, no detailed anatomy. The reticle rings are crisp geometric circles with crosshair lines.

Type notes: Metaphor speaks through irony — the "wrong" target. Viewers should instantly feel the reversal: the suited figures are inside the crosshair, not outside it.

Palette notes: Dark palette with high contrast accent. The coral-red targeting reticle pops dramatically against the near-black background. Creates tension and urgency. The muted blue-collar figures in safety zone reinforce the irony.

ASPECT: 2.35:1

Clean composition with generous dark breathing room. Targeting reticle as the undeniable focal point. Human figures: simplified stylized silhouettes only, not photorealistic. No clutter, no decorative noise.
